Funnel Cakes For Everyone

Description

I know the food from Hearthstone lol. The walrus guy shouted "Funnel cakes, Funnel Cakes for everyone!"

Ingredients

  • 1 quart vegetable oil for frying, or as needed
  • ½ cups milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup confectioners' sugar

Steps

  1. Heat oil to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) in a heavy skillet.
  2. Beat milk and eggs together in a large bowl. Combine flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt in a separate bowl; stir into egg mixture until smooth.
  3. Use your finger to cover the funnel hole; pour in 1 cup of batter. Starting in the center of the skillet, remove your finger and move the funnel in a swirling motion to make a 6- or 7-inch round.
  4. Fry in hot oil until golden brown, 1 to 2 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels.
  5. Sprinkle with confectioners' sugar and serve warm.
